SweetSpotter

A no-tech, open-source golf impact trainer. Stick it. Spot it. Improve.

A quick summary for those in a hurry:

  • ✅ Uses foam balls and tacky putty to visualize strike location
  • ✅ Indoor-friendly, safe, and beginner-approved
  • ✅ Fully open source under CC BY 4.0
